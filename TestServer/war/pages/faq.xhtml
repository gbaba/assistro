How does Assistro work?

Assistro is live chat software that enables website owners to engage with their website visitors in real time. After installing our code on your site, assistro will begin working immediately. When a customer comes to your website, the assistro chat window will automatically appear after 10 seconds inviting them to engage with you. If your customers have questions, they can respond to the proactive chat message and that will initiate a live chat session with one of your operators.

As your customer browses through your website the chat message will appear on each page so they can continue to chat with the operator and receive assistance as required.

What do my visitors need to for them to use Assistro?

Absolutely nothing! Well, a modern web browser does help (modern browsers are JavaScript enabled) but other than that they just need to visit your site and experience the awesomeness of your customer service now that you have assistro on your team.

Does Assistro support my language characters set?

The answer is YES. Assistro enables you to chat with your visitors/customers using any UTF-8 characters

Currently, the assistro site is only available in English, however once installed, there is no limitation on which language characters you can use to chat with your web visitors.

What instant messenger software should I use with Assistro?

One of assistro’s strengths is that we operate through Google Talk. Many of our customers simply use the Google Talk chat widgets, but we prefer to use messenger programs such as eBuddy or Pidgin – especially useful if you have assistro installed on multiple sites.

I thought Assistro is automatic but why when I visit the Website it does not automatically pop up?

Perhaps the agents are offline. When it is on Offline Mode, it will not open unless it is clicked. Otherwise, it automatically pops up after ten (10) seconds.

What if I want to close the chat window?

Assistro’s chat window pops up after ten (10) seconds of someone being on the site. Your visitors have the option to respond, ignore, minimize or close it. Assistro no longer prompts those who have already closed the chat box on your Website.

Can I put Assistro on more than one (1) Website?

Absolutely! You can copy/paste your assistro code on to as many web sites as you like. You're only limited by how many chat request you can handle at one time.

I can’t be in the office all the time, what happens when I’m mobile?

We get asked this question a lot. One of the biggest advantages of using assistro is the ability to chat with web visitors from your smartphone or tablet device. Simply visit the appstore or Android store and download one of the many Google Talk apps (we recommend eBuddy or Pidgin) and you can then send and receive chat messages while on the go!

I can’t be online all the time, what happens when I’m offline?

When all your operators are offline, assistro is offline. The offline mode will display the assistro widget in a minimized window on your website. Your web visitors can still choose to send you an email via assistro when you are offline so that you do not miss out on any potential enquiries.

What browser should I and my visitors use with Assistro?

You just need to download an application from iTunes or from the Android Store. We recommend eBuddy or Pidgin, or Google’s own Gtalk! App.
